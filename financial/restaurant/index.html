Restaurant Real Time Payments (RTP) Demo

This is a brief API journey, where a restaurant owner is needing to make an order for goods from one of their regular suppliers. Replenishing their inventory so that they can continue serving dinner for the rest of the week. Providing our catalyst for an everyday business tractions that occurs millions of times around the world on any given day, while thoroughly demonstrating the importance of API infrastructure. This is intended to be a brief demonstration of what is possible when Axway API management and choreography solutions, along with Streamdata.io event-driven infrastructure is employed within the banking industry, to meet the needs of everyday business owners.



A Restaurant Owner Needs To Place An Order With A Supplier

A restaurant owner is need of some supplies to keep his business running through the week, choosing to use of his existing providers who he knows has the items he is looking for.


Knowing that he will be able to make an real time payment through his bank, which will expedite the delivery coming from his supplier, and bringing what he needs to stay in business.
